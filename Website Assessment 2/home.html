About us

Big City Garage was established in 2014, we work on a variety of vehicles, including British, American, and German automobiles.

We offer a wide range of services for your car, from routine maintenance to complete gearbox and engine rebuilds.

Our bodywork crew is skilled and knowledgeable in all aspects of painting and bodywork from denting and fabrication to painting.

We also work on classic cars, performing complete top-to-bottom restorations as needed by the customer.
