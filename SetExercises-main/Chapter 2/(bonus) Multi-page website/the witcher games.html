  • The Witcher Games:

The Witcher is a series of fantasy action role-playing games developed by CD Projekt Red and published by CD Projekt. It is based on the book series of the same name by Polish writer Andrzej Sapkowski, acting as sequels to the story of the books.
You play as Geralt Of Rivia, A monster hunter for hire. In the game he embarks on an epic journey to find his former apprentice before The Wild Hunt can capture her and bring about the destruction of the world.
The witcher, Geralt of Rivia, in the search for his Yennefer of Vengerberg, learns that his former witcher apprentice Cirilla, who disappeared years ago, has returned to Temeria and is being chased by The Wild Hunt. Geralt then sets on a quest to find Cirilla before The Wild Hunt gets to her first.
